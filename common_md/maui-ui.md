````md
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
````

## Sequnce

1. **Single value:** `Margin="10"` = 10 pixels on all sides
1. **Two values:** `Margin="10,20"` = 10 horizontal (left/right), 20 vertical (top/bottom)
1. **Four values:** `Margin="5,10,15,20"` = Left=5, Top=10, Right=15, Bottom=20
